    • Matthew

    Matthew is a determined and driven individual who wants to build technology that makes people's lives easier. He is especially interested in creating software to help people learn and practice skills without having to worry about making their own exercises. He believes that playing games is a great way to reinforce concepts in people's minds and is interested in games in general, so he enjoys playing and developing those as well.

    When not working with technology, he tries to learn new skills and subjects to be exposed to other types of thinking. He believes that incorporating different patterns of thought into one's main line of work can give birth to interesting new ideas. In his free time, he tries to learn new skills to broaden his worldview.

    Inspirations

    America on Tech

    America on Tech eased me into the tech world and gave me access to many opportunities, including Bootcamps, Fellowships, and professional development opportunities. I especially enjoyed working for them as a Peer Mentor. Their noble mission has inspired me to look towards how to use coding for the good of the community.

    Brain Age

    I played Brain Age very frequently when I was younger, and I believe it contributed to the academic success I have today. It also serves as a great model for the type of games I want to create in the future: fun minigames that help train your brain.

    Duolingo

    DuoLingo does a good job at making learning fun by combining studying with participating in fun activities. Their excerises provide good practice for language learners.
